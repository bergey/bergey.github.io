Conductive Dough

  • 1 Cup Water
  • ½ Cup Flour
  • ¼ Cup Salt
  • ½ Cup Lemon Juice
  • 1 Tbsp. Vegetable Oil
  • Mix all ingredients in a pot
  • Cook over medium heat, stirring continuously, until chunky.
  • Remove from heat, stir until dough reaches uniform consistency
  • Let cool
  • If dough is sticky or oily, knead in additional flour

Insulating Dough

  • 1 ¼ Cup Flour
  • ½ Cup Sugar
  • 3 Tbsp. Vegetable Oil
  • ¼ Cup Filtered or Distilled Water
  • Food Coloring
  • Mix flour, sugar, and oil in a large bowl
  • Add food coloring to water
  • Mix in 1 Tbsp of water at a time.
  • Add more water or flour as neccessary to reach desired consistency.
