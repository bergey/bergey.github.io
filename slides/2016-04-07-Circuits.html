Tips for Reliable Circuits

  • Fold tape instead of cutting
  • Tape LEDs on top of copper with clear tape

Series Circuits

  • 2 LEDs
  • 2 Coin Cells

Parallel Circuit

  • 2 LEDs
  • 1 Coin Cell - will run down in half the time

Resistors & Math

V = IR ↔ R = V/I

  • 3V Coin Cell
  • 2V LED
  • < 15 mA LED (0.015 V)
  • (3V-2V) / 0.015A = 66Ω
  • (6V-4V) / 0.015A = 133Ω
  • (6V-2V) / 0.0.15A = 266Ω
